Fund Selection

Why your fund choice matters

The difference between being in a conservative fund vs a growth fund over 30 years can be $200,000–$400,000 or more. Most people were defaulted into a fund when they first started working and have never reviewed it. We help you understand your options and switch to the right fund for your age and goals.

Fund Type Risk Level Expected Long-term Return Best Suited To
Conservative Low ~3–4% p.a. Near retirement (within 5 years)
Balanced Medium ~5–6% p.a. Mid-career
Growth Medium-High ~6–8% p.a. Long-term investors (10+ years)
Aggressive High ~7–9% p.a. Young investors with 20+ years

Common Questions

Quick answers

Is KiwiSaver advice free?

Yes, same commission-based model as our insurance advice. Our review costs you nothing and you're under no obligation. See our full FAQ →

Can you switch providers for me?

Yes, any time. You can switch KiwiSaver providers whenever you like. If we recommend a switch, we handle all the paperwork. It typically takes 2-3 weeks to complete.

What's the government contribution?

Eligible members receive a government contribution each year. You just need to contribute a minimum amount yourself in the KiwiSaver year to qualify for the full amount. Rates are updated by the government, so check IRD.govt.nz for the current figures. Most people don't realise they qualify or how to maximise it.
