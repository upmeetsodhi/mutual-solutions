Nature of advice

We provide personalised financial advice on risk insurance (life, income protection, health, critical illness, disability), KiwiSaver, investments, and home loans.

Limitations

Our advice is limited to the products and providers we work with. We do not provide advice on general insurance (home and contents, vehicle), legal advice, or tax advice.

Fees, Expenses & Commissions

Our financial advice is provided at no direct cost to you. We receive commissions from insurance companies and lenders when you take out a policy or loan through us. Commission rates vary by provider and product.

We will disclose the specific commission applicable to any recommendation we make to you. This commission does not affect the cost of your insurance or loan.

If any fees apply in special circumstances, we will tell you before you are obligated to pay.

Conflicts of Interest

Mutual Solutions Limited receives commissions from insurance companies and lenders. To manage potential conflicts of interest:

  1. We always recommend the product we believe best suits your needs.
  2. We maintain an approved product list of reputable providers.
  3. Our internal advice processes require us to document and justify every recommendation.

A list of the providers we work with includes: Partners Life, AIA, Fidelity Life, Asteron Life, nib, Southern Cross, Cigna, BNZ, ANZ, Westpac.

Privacy Policy

We collect personal information to provide financial advice. This may include your name, contact details, financial situation, health information, and goals. We collect this directly from you.

How we use your information

  • To provide personalised financial advice.
  • To place insurance or loan applications on your behalf.
  • To maintain records as required by law.

Your rights

We store information securely and do not sell it to third parties. You have the right to access and correct information we hold about you. Contact us at info@mutuals.co.nz to do so.

We comply with the Privacy Act 2020 (New Zealand).
