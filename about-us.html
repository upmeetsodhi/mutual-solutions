About Us

Kiwi families deserve better advice.

Mutual Solutions is an independent insurance and financial advice business based in Palmerston North, serving clients across New Zealand since 2012. We are not owned by, or tied to, any bank or insurer.

Our Story

Independent from day one

Mutual Solutions Limited was founded in Palmerston North in 2012 with a simple idea: insurance and financial advice works better when the adviser has no reason to favour one provider over another. Over 10+ years, that's meant helping hundreds of families and business owners across the lower North Island, and increasingly nationwide by phone and video, get cover that actually fits their life rather than whatever a bank or insurer's own sales targets pushed that quarter.

We're a registered Financial Advice Provider (FSP 729871), and every recommendation we make is disclosed, documented, and built around your goals, your budget, and your family, not our margins.
