About Mutual Solutions

Kiwi families deserve better advice.

Mutual Solutions Limited is a registered Financial Advice Provider (FSP 729871) based in Palmerston North, serving families and business owners across the Manawatū region and throughout New Zealand. We've been providing independent financial and insurance advice for over 10 years — and our sole focus is on what's best for you.

We're not owned by or tied to any bank or insurer. That means every recommendation we make is based on your goals, your budget, and your life — not our margins. Whether you need life insurance, income protection, health cover, KiwiSaver advice, or a home loan, we compare the whole market to find the right fit.

Life Insurance

If you were gone tomorrow, would your family be okay financially? Life cover ensures your loved ones can maintain their lifestyle, clear debt, and plan ahead — without financial stress on top of grief.

→

02

Income Protection

Your income is your most valuable asset. If illness or injury stops you working, income protection replaces up to 75% of your earnings — so your mortgage, bills, and lifestyle are covered.

→

03

Health Insurance

Skip the public waiting list. Private health cover gives you fast access to specialists and elective procedures — and the freedom to choose your own surgeon and hospital.

→

04

Critical Illness

A lump-sum payout on diagnosis of cancer, heart attack, stroke, or other serious conditions — giving you financial freedom to focus entirely on your treatment and recovery.

→

05

Disability Cover

Total and permanent disability cover pays a lump-sum benefit if you're permanently unable to work — helping fund ongoing care costs, debt clearance, and home modifications.

→

06

Level Premiums

Tired of premiums rising every year? Level premium policies lock in your rate — making cover more predictable and often much cheaper over the long term.

→

07

Home Loans

First home buyer or refinancing? We navigate the mortgage market across multiple lenders to find you the structure and rate that actually fits your situation — no bank bias.

→

08

KiwiSaver

Most Kiwis are in the wrong KiwiSaver fund. We review your current fund, model your retirement outlook, and move you to a fund matched to your goals and risk appetite.

→

09

Investments

Growing your wealth takes more than just saving — it takes a plan. We assess your goals, time horizon, and risk profile to recommend an investment strategy that works for you, whether you're just starting out or looking to grow an existing portfolio.
