About

10+ Years of Independent Advice

With over a decade of experience, I've helped hundreds of New Zealand families make confident financial decisions. My approach is simple: independent, unbiased advice tailored to your situation.

I'm not tied to any product provider, which means my recommendations are genuinely in your best interest. Whether it's protecting your income, optimizing your KiwiSaver, or finding the right home loan, I take time to understand your goals before suggesting solutions.

Based in Palmerston North but serving clients nationwide, I combine personal service with access to a nationwide network of specialists and lenders.
